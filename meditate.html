Minecraft model creation and ideation

When I was working on this project, I began to notice that I was much more calm doing art in a digital space when making projects through creative outlets like minecraft rather than any other stressful project I've done.

This made me come up with the idea of meditation in a digital space using art and more traditional aspects of what peace can possibly look like.

I created a 3d model of one of my favorite original characters doing what she normally doesn't do: Meditation. Throughout her painful existence this moment that she lives in is one of the only peaceful ones she's ever known.

Final product

In the final product, I used something deeply personal to me- neon brigjt colors and painted flowers upon a sketchbook to display her.

The neon colors are meant to show the nuances of creativity and how they relate to our mind's imagination, and the flower bed is laid out in front of her to showcase her tranquility in this moment.
